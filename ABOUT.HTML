About Blockchain Cabs

Gamifying the new collaborative economy

All over the world, evolving technologies are helping to speed up power transfer between centralized institutions and distributed networks driven by voluntary, peer-to-peer collaboration.

As a way of hastening this trend, at Blockchain Cabs, we are working towards disrupting and decentralizing the highest-valued privately-held corporation in the globe: Uber.

In an effort to achieve this, plans are in place provide a crystal-clear and transparent information to every party involved in the ridesharing transaction. Our aim is to incentivize the act of information sharing through gamification. We will also organize community structures to help in creating local replacements for the usual corporate structures.

Powered by Ethereum

We recently issued our Ethereum-based Blockchain Token and launched a community incentive to our esteemed network consisting of over 70,000+ passengers and drivers.

Plans are in place for our mobile app to feature a deep integration of Blockchain Cabs Tokens into the interphase of the app. The Blockchain Cabs Token can be used at will. However, the tokens are a requirement if you will like to partake in the aforementioned gamification layer.

As the Ethereum network continues growing in capacity for production apps at scale, Blockchain Cabs will one of the first applications to implement cutting edge ideas ranging from tokenized reputation systems to self-sovereign identity, decentralized storage, and lots more. All of these will be tied back to our Blockchain Cabs Token. Watch Out!
